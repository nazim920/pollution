Interactive Air Quality Analysis

Date:

January 23, 2025

Task:

Developing an interactive visualization tool for air quality analysis.

Operations:

  • Interactive Map Navigation: Hover over regions to see pollutant levels.
  • Select specific pollutants for focused visualization.
  • Zoom in to explore city-level data.
  • Use a timeline slider to view historical trends.
  • Download filtered datasets for further analysis.

Details:

Data Needed:

Air quality data by region and city (PM2.5, PM10, NO2).

GeoJSON data for country boundaries.

Algorithms:

  • Data preprocessing for pollutant mapping.
  • Outlier detection for erroneous data.
  • Interactive tooltip generation for user engagement.

Metrics:

  • Regional pollution averages.
  • Normalized pollution levels by students density.

Layout:

A world map interface with interactive features:

  • Regions color-coded based on PM2.5 levels.
  • Pollutant-specific toggling options.
  • Zoom-in feature for exploring cities/regions.
  • Tooltips with pollutant averages and contextual data.

Focus:

The tool highlights:

  • Multi-pollutant visualization for detailed comparisons.
  • Historical trends by region or globally.
  • Health impact context for vulnerable studdents.
